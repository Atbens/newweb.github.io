Roadmap

We’re in this for the long haul.
We’ve set up a timeline for ourselves and will work on realizing the stated goal in order.

"Initial Construction"

We are working hard to build the community, and give 371 whitelist free minting places to those who initially participated in community building, 740 presalelist places to those who participated in community building early.

"Public Sale"

A total of 10,000 Emoji NFTs are available for public sale. Each Emoji NFT costs 0.015ETH. Let's go!

"The Bar"

Building an exclusive bar for the club, and those who hold Emoji NFTs will get membership. This bar can be used by members to chat, socialize and design new emojis. We will create a leaderboard for Emoji based on the number of times they are used in the club. Community airdrops are distributed to NFT holders every month according to the leaderboard.

"Token"

Community governance tokens will be introduced, and who holds Emoji NFT will be able to receive 10,000 token airdrops. Simultaneously, Token staking will be introduced which will allow people to earn in either short or long term.

"The Scanner"

Each Emoji is supported by a skull, and the skull cannot be observed only from the appearance. So we' build The Scanner. Each holder of Emoji NFT can scan their own NFT and find the skull hidden in Emoji. Note that some skulls are connected to the skulls of our game characters.

"NFTs For Game"

Character NFTs that launch our pirate gambling game. These character NFTs will be divided into regular and rare versions. The total supply of rare NFTs are 10,000. The normal version of NFT will be released in batches and in unlimited quantities at a low price, in order to lower the game threshold and give everyone a fair chance to join our game. As long as you hold the property NFT, you can enter the game.
